# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

> 本仓库遵循 [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)；中文译文与英文等效，正式约束以英文为准。

## Our Pledge · 我们的承诺

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

我们作为社区成员、贡献者和领导者，承诺无论年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残疾、
族裔、性别特征、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育背景、社会经济状况、国籍、
个人外貌、种姓、肤色、宗教、性身份与取向如何，所有人在本社区的参与都
不会受到骚扰。

我们致力于以促进开放、欢迎、多元、包容、健康社区的方式来行为与互动。

## Our Standards · 行为标准

### Positive · 鼓励的行为

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people · 对他人表现同理与善意
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences · 尊重不同的观点、立场和经验
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback · 主动给出并坦然接受建设性反馈
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes · 为错误负责并向受影响者道歉
- Focusing on what is best for the overall community · 以社区整体利益为出发点

### Unacceptable · 不可接受的行为

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind · 使用性化语言/图像、做出任何形式的性暗示或求欢
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks · 钓鱼、侮辱、贬低性言论以及人身攻击或政治攻击
- Public or private harassment · 公开或私下的骚扰
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without their explicit permission · 未经许可公开他人地址、邮箱等私人信息
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting · 其他在专业场合中可被合理视为不恰当的行为

## Enforcement Responsibilities · 执行责任

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of
acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in
response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive,
or harmful.

社区维护者负责明确并执行行为标准，对其认为不恰当、具威胁性、冒犯或有害的
行为采取合适且公正的纠正措施，包括移除评论、Commits、代码、Wiki 编辑、
Issues 等不符合本准则的贡献，必要时临时或永久封禁参与者。

## Scope · 适用范围

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when
an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples
of representing our community include using an official email address, posting
via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative
at an online or offline event.

本准则适用于所有社区空间；当个人代表社区在公开场所发声时同样适用。

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainer at:

- **GitHub**: [@xiadengma](https://github.com/xiadengma)
- **Private Vulnerability Reporting** (for behavior tied to a security incident):
  https://github.com/xiadengma/ai-intervention-agent/security/advisories/new

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All
community leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the

如遇到滥用、骚扰或其他不可接受行为，请通过上述渠道私下联系维护者。所有
投诉都将被及时、公正地审阅与处理；维护者有义务保护举报人的隐私与安全。

## Enforcement Guidelines · 处置指引

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining
the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct:

### 1. Correction · 提醒

**Community Impact**: Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed
unprofessional or unwelcome.

**Consequence**: A private, written warning from community leaders, providing
clarity around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the
behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning · 警告

**Community Impact**: A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

**Consequence**: A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No
interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with
those enforcing the Code of Conduct, for a specified period of time.

### 3. Temporary Ban · 临时封禁

**Community Impact**: A serious violation of community standards, including
sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence**: A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public
communication with the community for a specified period of time.

### 4. Permanent Ban · 永久封禁

**Community Impact**: Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards,
including sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment of an individual, or
aggression toward or disparagement of classes of individuals.

**Consequence**: A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the
community.
